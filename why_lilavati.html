Why choose Us?

The formation of the LILAWATI Foundation is determined by a deep passion for addressing critical issues related to child and women, such as poverty, education, healthcare, and human rights. Inspired by personal experiences and witnessing injustices and hardships, the foundation aims to create meaningful social changes and raise awareness to empower needy person to make informed decisions.

Our Commitments

• Passion: The foundation is driven by a genuine passion for social justice and a commitment to making a difference.

• Personal Connection: Founders and supporters have personal experiences that motivate their involvement.

• Comprehensive Approach: Addressing a wide range of issues from poverty to global health, the foundation adopts a holistic approach to social change.

• Community-Centric: Focusing on the needs of communities ensures that the foundation's efforts are relevant and impactful.

• Global Vision: With an eye on both national and international issues, the foundation aims to create widespread positive change.

• Collaborative Spirit: By fostering collaboration, the foundation amplifies its impact through partnerships.
